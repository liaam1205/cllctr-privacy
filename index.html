Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 February 2026

Cllctr is a collectibles tracking app developed by Midnight Studios. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

All collection data — including item names, prices, notes, and images — is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store this data on our servers.

Search Queries

When you search for items, your search query is sent to our server (hosted on Railway) to fetch pricing data from eBay and Discogs. These queries contain no personally identifiable information.

Billing

In-app purchases are handled by RevenueCat and Google Play. We do not process or store any payment information. Please refer to RevenueCat's privacy policy and Google's privacy policy for details.

Third Party Services

  • eBay Browse API — product search and pricing
  • Discogs API — vinyl record pricing
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing

Children's Privacy

Cllctr does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at cllctrofficial@gmail.com
