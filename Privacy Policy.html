Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 March 2026

Cllctr is a collectibles tracking app developed by Midnight Studios. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

All collection data — including item names, prices, notes, and images — is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store this data on our servers.

Search & Pricing Queries

When you search for items, look up a barcode, or the app fetches updated market prices, your search query is sent to our backend server (hosted on Railway) to retrieve pricing data from eBay. These queries contain no personally identifiable information.

Currency Conversion

When you change your currency setting, the app fetches the latest exchange rates from open.er-api.com. No personal data is included in these requests.

Billing

In-app purchases are handled by RevenueCat and Google Play. We do not process or store any payment information. Please refer to RevenueCat's privacy policy and Google's privacy policy for details.

Third-Party Services

  • eBay Browse API — product search and pricing (via our Railway backend)
  • Discogs API — vinyl record search and marketplace pricing (via our Railway backend)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing
  • open.er-api.com — currency exchange rates

Children's Privacy

Cllctr does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
