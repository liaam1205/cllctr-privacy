Privacy Policy for Cllctr
Last updated: 28 February 2026
Cllctr ("the app") is developed by Midnight Studios. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.
Data Storage
All collection data, including item names, prices, notes, and images, is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this data on our servers.
Search Queries
When you search for items, the app sends your search query to our server (hosted on Railway) to fetch pricing data from eBay and Discogs. These queries contain no personally identifiable information.
Billing
In-app purchases are handled by RevenueCat and Google Play. We do not process or store any payment information. Please refer to RevenueCat's and Google Play's privacy policies for details on how they handle your payment data.
Third Party Services
The app uses the following third party services: eBay Browse API for product search and pricing, Discogs API for vinyl pricing, RevenueCat for subscription management, and Google Play Billing.
Children's Privacy
The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
Contact
For any privacy questions, contact us at cllctrofficial@gmail.com
